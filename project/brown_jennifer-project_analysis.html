Site Intent:

The goals for the site re-design is to make this clinic more appealing to anyone in need of a veterinarian clinic for there furry family member(s). It is to be organized in a manner that is easy to understand by all ages, and for new customers to find the information that they require in a timely organized fashion.

Currently there is so much going on within the site that it looks congested. It is through this congestion and no clear direction that people(i.e. new customers) could become easily confused or frustrated within the site. The site should represent how the clinic works and through the current site it gives the persona of disarray, unorganized, clustered and not very clear meaning of what the clinic is trying to provide for there current and potential customers.

I feel that the new site will give a new customer that calm feeling knowing that there is a clinic that will take care of there family member with the upmost care and love. It is through the goals of the site that there will be a navigation bar on the top that clearly delineates specifics like: Home, Services, About Us, and Clinic Information. Then there will be a clean header and footer that represents the clinic's orderly fashion. The header of the page will include the Logo, and navigational menu. The color pallet for the page will be more representative of the services rendered and to bring in customers with a calm color pallet. It is through this concept that we will bring in more customers and help them to feel at ease regarding anything that need to happen for there pet.

Kim's cat goes through surgery, and Kim is extremely happy and refers all of her family and friends to Kirkman Road Veterinary Services for there extreme attention to detail, ability to calm her down in a crisis and there overall level of care given to her cat.
